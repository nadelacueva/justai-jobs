JustAI Jobs

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is JustAI Jobs?

JustAI Jobs is a platform that connects individuals seeking work in the field of ethical AI with organizations that are hiring responsibly. It is built as a course project aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

2. Is JustAI Jobs a real job board?

No, JustAI Jobs is a course project. While the platform is functional, it is created for educational purposes and may not provide the same level of service or support as a professional platform.

3. Who can register on the platform?

Anyone who is 18 years or older and interested in ethical AI work can register. Whether you’re a job seeker or an employer, we welcome your participation.

4. How do I apply for jobs?

After registering and creating your profile, you can browse job listings and apply directly through the platform.

5. Are the jobs on JustAI Jobs verified?

Since this is a course project, job listings may be simulated or used for demonstration purposes. While we aim to promote authenticity, verification is not guaranteed.

6. How is my data used?

Your data is used solely for connecting with job opportunities and will not be sold or rented to third parties. For more, see our Terms and Conditions.

7. Can I delete my account?

Yes, you may request account deletion through the Support & Inquiries page. We’ll handle it promptly.

8. What kind of support is available?

Since JustAI Jobs is a course project, support is limited. However, we provide a Support & Inquiries page for reaching out with questions or issues.

9. Will JustAI Jobs become a real platform?

While it is currently an academic project, there's potential to grow depending on user interest and feedback.
